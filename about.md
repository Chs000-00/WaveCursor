# Wave Cursor

Changes your in-game <cy>cursor</c> to <cr>match</c> your **<cd>wave icon!</c>**  
This mod also adds your **<cj>trail</c>** to the <cy>cursor</c>.

## Notes

There is an <cr>issue</c> where the cursor trail does not show up when starting the game. Restarting the game should fix it.
